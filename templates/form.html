Welcome to the Academic Performance Indicator

This tool is designed to provide hypothetical insights into the complex factors influencing academic success. It utilizes a fictional dataset, not based on real individuals or groups, as a means to foster discussion and awareness around educational equity. The goal is to use this simulated model to highlight potential inequities in education and to encourage dialogue about fair resource distribution in schools. Remember, this is a starting point for conversations about educational policy reform and not an assessment of any individual's potential.
